# COMPLETE FEAT TREES

## Tier I Feats

### Rogue
- **Shadow Step** — Mobility system; dash grants brief invisibility (1.5 seconds).  

### Rogue/Wizard
- **Phantom Strike** — Cloak + energy weapon; attacks from stealth deal bonus energy damage.  

### Rogue/Cleric
- **Surgical Precision** — Critical hits restore small amount of ally health within 10 meters.  
- **Veil of Mercy** — Cloak system + support reactor; stealth applies brief ally damage reduction aura.  

### Wizard
- **Beam Focus** — Energy weapon equipped; standing still increases beam damage by 15%.  

### Wizard/Cleric
- **Radiant Overflow** — Energy reactor + support system; excess energy creates small heal-over-time zone.  
- **Empowered Blessing** — Energy weapon + buff system; weapon hits extend active buff durations by 0.5s.  

### Cleric
- **Reactive Healing**  
  *Requirement:* Support reactor equipped  
  *Description:* When an ally within 15 meters drops below 30% HP, automatically pulse heal them for 15% HP.  
  *Trait:* Cleric  
  *Cooldown:* 20 seconds per ally

## Tier V Feats

### Rogue
- **Shadow Master** — Cloak duration doubled; can attack 3 times before breaking stealth.  

### Rogue/Wizard
- **Quantum Assassin** — Phase-shift + cloak + energy weapon; chain teleport between 5 targets, striking each once with guaranteed criticals.  
- **Phantom Artillery** — Cloak system + beam weapon; enter stealth, plant immobile turret mode, fire sustained beam at +200% damage while invisible.  

### Rogue/Cleric
- **Divine Veil** — Deploy zone that cloaks all allies and provides immunity to status effects for 8 seconds.  
- **Angel of Death** — Stealth kills heal all allies for 40% HP and grant +50% damage for 10 seconds.  

### Wizard
- **Arcane Annihilation**  
  *Requirement:* Energy reactor equipped; beam weapon installed  
  *Description:* Channel full energy pool into single beam that pierces all targets in line, dealing 800% damage.  
  *Trait:* Wizard  
  *Cooldown:* 120 seconds  

### Wizard/Cleric
- **Infinite Wellspring** — Energy overflow + support system; while above 80% energy, constantly heal all allies within 30 meters.  
- **Empowered Singularity** — Energy reactor + buff system; deploy zone that amplifies all ally damage by 60% for 12 seconds.  

### Cleric/Wizard
- **Radiant Apocalypse** — Support reactor + energy weapon; fire beam that heals allies and damages enemies simultaneously in path.  

### Cleric
- **Martyrdom Transcendent**  
  *Requirement:* Support reactor equipped; martyrdom system installed  
  *Description:* For 15 seconds, redirect ALL ally damage within 30 meters to self. Damage taken this way reduces self HP to minimum 1, cannot kill. After duration, heal self for 50% of damage absorbed.  
  *Trait:* Cleric  
  *Cooldown:* 180 seconds
