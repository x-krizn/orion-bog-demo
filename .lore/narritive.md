**Grail Sickness Mechanic:**

**State 1: Neutral (25% baseline)**
- NPC will trade, provide dialogue
- Grail sickness symptoms visible but controlled

**State 2: Agitated (50% probability)**
- NPC dialogue becomes hostile

- Drops standard loot

**State 3: Feral (25% probability)**
- Immediate aggro on sight
- Enhanced stats (+25% damage, +50% movement speed)

- Orc raid invasion forces ALL NPCs into Feral state for duration

### BOSS ENCOUNTER DESIGN

**Percival, Tristan, Bors** (3 Mini-Bosses, Can Fight Individually or Together)

**Percival** - Grail Knight (Heavy Tank)
- **Resource Priority:** Threat > Energy > Heat
- **Behavior:** Guardian-archetype, protects Tristan/Bors if fought together
- **Mechanics:**
  - Shield wall stance: reduces damage, reflects projectiles
  - Grail Regeneration: passive HP regen when below 50%
  - Righteous Charge: gap-closer with knockback
- **Loot:** Guardian Frame Blueprint + "Grail Fragment" (quest item)

**Tristan** - Corrupted Ranger (DPS/Ranged)
- **Resource Priority:** Energy > Heat > Threat
- **Behavior:** Kiting, maintains distance, punishes melee rushdown
- **Mechanics:**
  - Longbow Volley: high-damage ranged attack pattern
  - Smoke Retreat: teleports to edge of arena when cornered
  - Grail Sight: reveals player position through walls
- **Loot:** Advanced Sensor Suite + "Corrupted Shard" (crafting material)

**Bors** - Berserk Striker (High-Risk Aggro)
- **Resource Priority:** Heat > Threat > Energy
- **Behavior:** Marauder-archetype, relentless aggression
- **Mechanics:**
  - Fury Stacks: gains +10% damage per hit landed (max 10 stacks)
  - Grail Overload: at 50% HP, enters overdrive (immune to heat penalties)
  - Execution Lunge: one-shot ability if player below 20% HP
- **Loot:** Heavy Lance "Camlann's Ruin" + Armor Plating Blueprint

**Combined Fight Bonuses:**
- Fighting all 3 simultaneously grants "Knight's Oath" feat (+10% damage vs Camelot enemies)
- Unique dialogue sequence reveals lore about grail corruption

### ORC RAIDING PARTY SYSTEM

**Dynamic Invasion Trigger:**
- 15% chance every 5 minutes of exploration
- Announced by war horn sound + red visual pulse
- Blocks zone exit until cleared OR player escapes to dungeon entrance

**Invasion Composition:**
- 8-12 orc grunts (basic melee)
- 2-4 orc rangers (ranged harassment)
- 1 orc chieftain (mini-boss, drops map fragments)

**Tactical Layer:**
- Orcs prioritize attacking NPCs first
- Player can exploit chaos: loot NPC corpses, scavenge while enemies distracted
- Successfully defending town (killing all orcs before NPCs die) grants reputation bonus with surviving NPCs → temporary safezone effect
